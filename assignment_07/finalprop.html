Final Project Outline & Proposal

Topic

I wil be creating a promotional website for the country, Peru. I chose this as my topic because I am Peruvian and would like to not only learn more about my culture but create a page that I am proud to share.

Audience

My intended audience is anyone who is curious to learn more about Peru's history, culture, cuisine, landmarks, and my personal traveling tips.

Design & Style

The design of my website will follow nature and high saturated images that are colorful representations of Peru. The color scheme I will be using is based on the country's flag. The primary color will be red, seondary will be forrest green, and the remaining colors I may use are gold and turqoise. The websites that inspire my design choices are:

  • Peerspace: I love the large image layouts and scrolling
  • Native Poppy: I love the menu/navigation layout
  • Woolx: I love the muted video that plays automatically when you scroll and the general layout

Scope

I will use 3 pages for my website, not including the landing page. The landing, culture, and travel pages will each have 2 sections as well.
